### Server Requirements

Filepicker has a few requirements:

- PHP 5.3.3+
- [Fileinfo](https://php.net/manual/ro/book.fileinfo.php)
- [GD](http://php.net/manual/en/book.image.php)
- [Exif](http://php.net/manual/en/book.exif.php)
